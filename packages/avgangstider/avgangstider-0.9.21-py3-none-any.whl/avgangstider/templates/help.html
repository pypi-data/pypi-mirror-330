As a minimum, you need to provide a stop_id. You can find your stop_id in two ways:
  1. Browse the interactive map at entur.no. Click the station you are interested in, and then click "Se alle avganger" in the window that pops up. The stop_id will now be part of your URL: Look for something like NSR:StopPlace:59651.
  2. Search the National Stop Register.

Examples

  • Jernbanetorget (NSR:StopPlace:58366)
  • Jernbanetorget noen linjer (NSR:StopPlace:58366)
  • Oslo S (NSR:StopPlace:59872)
  • Torget i Bergen (NSR:StopPlace:59838)
  • Prinsens gate (Trondheim) (NSR:StopPlace:41613)
  • Next 5 departures from Godlia T, only in direction towards city center (NSR:StopPlace:5968)
